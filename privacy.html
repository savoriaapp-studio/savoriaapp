Privacy Policy

Savoria — Last updated: July 2, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what data Savoria ("the app", "we") collects, how it is used, and how you can delete it. Savoria is an AI-powered recipe app for Android, published by an independent developer.

1. Data we collect

DataWhenPurpose
Google account information (email address, display name) When you sign in with Google Authentication and syncing your data across your devices
Your content: recipes, shopping lists, food addresses, kitchen equipment settings As you use the app while signed in Storing and syncing your data (Google Firebase Firestore)
Images (recipe photos generated by AI) When a recipe or address image is generated Displaying your content (Google Firebase Storage)
Messages, photos and documents you submit to AI features When you use the AI chef, recipe import or cooking assistant Processed by the Google Gemini API to generate the response. Not used to build a profile of you.
AI usage counters (number of requests per day) When you use AI features Abuse prevention and fair-use quotas

2. What we do NOT do

  • We do not sell or rent your data to anyone.
  • We do not show ads and do not use advertising trackers.
  • We do not use your recipes, messages or images for marketing or profiling.

3. Guest mode (no account)

If you choose to use Savoria without signing in, your recipes and lists are stored locally on your device only and are never uploaded to our servers. AI requests are still processed through our server and the Google Gemini API, associated with an anonymous technical identifier, but no personal information (name, email) is collected.

4. Sharing with other users

Savoria lets you share your recipes, shopping lists and addresses with people you explicitly authorize using a share code. Shared data is only visible to the people you grant access to, and you can revoke access at any time in the app settings.

5. Third-party services

Savoria relies on the following services, operated by Google LLC:

  • Firebase Authentication — sign-in
  • Firebase Firestore & Firebase Storage — data and image storage
  • Google Gemini API / Vertex AI — AI text and image generation

Their processing is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

6. Data deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time:

  • In the app: Settings → Account → "Delete my account". Deletion is immediate and irreversible.
  • Online: submit a deletion request form. Requests are processed within 30 days and confirmed by email.

Deletion removes: your recipes, shopping lists, addresses, equipment settings, sharing profile, generated images, AI usage counters, and your sign-in account. Nothing personally identifiable is retained after deletion.

Guest mode data lives only on your device — uninstalling the app removes it.

7. Data security

Your data is stored on Google Firebase infrastructure, protected by per-user access rules: your data is only accessible to your authenticated account and to users you have explicitly granted sharing access to.

8. Children

Savoria is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from them.

9. Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the update will be published at this address with a new "Last updated" date.
