What is Savoria?

Savoria helps you collect, organize and cook your recipes with the help of AI:

  • Import recipes from any URL, plain text, photos or PDFs — automatically structured by AI
  • Chat with an AI chef for ideas, adaptations and cooking advice tailored to your kitchen equipment
  • Step-by-step cooking mode with per-step timers, ingredient reminders and a contextual AI assistant
  • Smart shopping list with automatic categorization and duplicate merging
  • A personal address book for your favorite food finds
  • Real-time sharing of recipes, shopping lists and addresses with people you choose
