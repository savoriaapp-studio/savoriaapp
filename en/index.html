Frequently asked questions

Everything you want to know.

Is Savoria free?
Yes. Savoria is available for free on Google Play during its open testing phase.
Can I import a recipe from Instagram, TikTok or YouTube?
Yes. Paste the link or share the post to Savoria: the AI Chef fetches the recipe and structures it automatically, even from a video.
Does Savoria adapt recipes to my oven and stovetop?
Yes. Set up your equipment (oven, stovetop, air fryer, food processors) once, and Savoria adapts the cooking steps to your actual gear.
Can I import a recipe from a photo or PDF?
Yes. Snap a handwritten recipe or a cookbook page, or import a PDF: Savoria extracts the ingredients and steps.
Which platforms is Savoria available on?
Savoria is available on Android via Google Play. An iOS version is under consideration.
How does cooking mode work?
Cooking mode shows the recipe step by step, with per-step timers, ingredient reminders and an AI assistant that knows the current recipe.
